## 💡 How to Score 80%+

### **For MCQ Questions (Easy)**
1. Read question carefully (often trick words like "NOT", "always", "never")
2. Eliminate obviously wrong answers
3. Choose best answer
4. Trust textbook concepts

### **For Structured Questions (Medium/Hard)**
1. **Answer the question asked** - don't just write facts
2. **Use key terms** from textbook (shows you understand)
3. **Explain WHY** not just WHAT (e.g., "because..." "this causes...")
4. **Use examples** when asked (e.g., dimples, copper wires)
5. **Show your thinking** in multi-step questions

### **PSLE Trick Questions - Watch Out For:**
- ❌ "Water vapor is visible" (NO - steam is visible, vapor is invisible)
- ❌ "Pollination = seed formation" (NO - fertilization must follow)
- ❌ "All bulbs stay bright when added in series" (NO - all get dimmer)
- ❌ "Heating water above 100°C makes it hotter" (NO - latent heat converts to evaporation)
